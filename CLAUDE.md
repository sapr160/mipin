# mipin

Unofficial, free web app for athletes at the Central American and Caribbean Games Santo Domingo 2026: pin-exchange marketplace (core) + swipe-matching. Next.js + Supabase + Vercel, bilingual ES (default)/EN. Never use official Games marks or imply affiliation.
