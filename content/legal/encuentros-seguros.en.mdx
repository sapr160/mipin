# Safe meetups

_Last updated: July 23, 2026._

mipin only makes introductions between athletes: **we do not verify anyone's identity.** If you decide to trade pins in person, use your head. These tips help keep the meetup safe.

## Before you meet

- **Meet in public venue areas.** Choose busy, common areas with people around, like the village, plazas, or spectator zones. Avoid secluded spots.
- **Tell a teammate.** Let someone on your team know who you're meeting, where, and when.
- **In daylight.** Prefer meeting during the day, when there's more activity around.

## During the meetup

- **Never share your room number** or where you're staying, and don't go to anyone else's.
- **Stay in the public area.** Make the trade in view of others and head back to your group.
- **Trust your instinct.** If something feels off, you don't have to stay: walk away and tell a teammate.

## Remember

mipin does not supervise WhatsApp conversations or in-person meetups. Any meetup happens at your own risk. If someone makes you feel unsafe, report them inside the app.
