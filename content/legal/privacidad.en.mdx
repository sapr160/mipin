# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 23, 2026._

This policy explains what data mipin stores, why, and how you can delete it. mipin is an independent, free project offered by the mipin team. The Spanish version is the official one and prevails over any translation.

## What data we store

- **Your account and profile:** the details you give us when you sign up and build your profile, such as your name or alias, your country or delegation, and your sport.
- **Your pins:** the pins you post as available and the ones you're looking for.
- **Your exchange activity:** the athletes you match with and the trades you agree to inside the app.
- **Your WhatsApp number:** with the special handling described below.
- **Reports and moderation:** if you or someone else files a report, we keep the report and a record of any suspension or ban.

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

## Your WhatsApp number

Your WhatsApp number is the most sensitive piece of data in mipin, so we handle it with extra care:

- **Collected late:** we don't ask for your number when you sign up. We only ask for it the first time you choose to reveal your contact to a match.
- **Hidden until mutual consent:** your number is not shown to anyone until **both people** have agreed to share contact. Without that mutual consent, it stays hidden.
- **Protected in the database:** access is locked down at the database level (row-level security), so only a mutually consented match can read your number.
- **Delete it yourself:** you can delete your account and data at any time from within the app; when you do, your number is deleted along with the rest of your information (a cascade delete).
- **Safety exception:** if you have been reported or banned, we may keep the minimum information needed — including your number where relevant — to enforce the action and protect other people, as an exception to deletion.

## Cookies

mipin uses only two cookies, none for advertising or tracking:

- **Language cookie (`NEXT_LOCALE`):** remembers whether you prefer Spanish or English. Lasts up to about 13 months.
- **First-touch source cookie:** records whether you arrived via a QR code or a shared link (`qr` or `share`). It's set on your first visit, is never overwritten afterward, and lasts up to about 13 months.

## How to delete your data

You can delete your account and all your data from within the app at any time. Deletion cascades: your profile, your pins, your matches, and your WhatsApp number are removed, except for the minimum information we must keep under the safety exception described above.

## Changes

We may update this policy. If we make significant changes, we'll update the date above.
